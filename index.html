Inside your forgotten belly button is an ecosystem where thousands of species are living. Some of those species can be only found so far in belly buttons and can help with producing products such as synthesizing beef meat. This dashboard is a product of documenting various people's belly buttons to discover those who carry such bacteria. For those who participated in this study can use drop down menu to their ID number. The graphs will show the top ten bacteria found in the bar graph above and all the bacteria found in a bubble chart below. All users may benefit from this dashboard as the demographics listed under the dropdown menu and the gauge will give insight to which candidates would produce which bacteria.
